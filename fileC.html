Arches National Park

This national park has the most density of sandstone arches. There are over than 2,000 natural sandstone arches, and is placed at 4,085 to 5,653 feet above water. April 12,1929 President Herbert Hoover signed presidential proclamarion No.1875 to make Acrches a National Park.

Things to do at Arches:
  • Biking
  • Camping
  • Rock climbing
  • Hiking

There are many trails for people to walk ranging from a 1 hour walk to a half-day walk.
