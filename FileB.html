Yellowstone National Park

In 1872 Yellowstone became the worlds first national park.

Facts about Yellowstone:
  • 2,219,789 acres
  • Has 12 campgrounds, over 2,000 campsites
  • Over 290 waterfalls

There are many of fun activites the park offers:

  • Hiking
  • boating
  • Horseback Riding
  • Fishing
