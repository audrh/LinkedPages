Grand Canyon National Park

The Grand Canyon is known to be one of the most beautiful and awe inspiring places on Earth. It was formed long ago by a massive glacier that cut through the landscape. In 1919, the Grand Canyon was officially designated as a national park. In 1903, President Theodore Roosevelt visited the site and said: "The Grand Canyon fills me with awe.
